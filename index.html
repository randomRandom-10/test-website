The College of Social Science and Philosophy (CSSP) is an integral part of the academic landscape of the University of the Philippines Diliman. Located in Palma Hall, CSSP houses the following departments: Anthropology, Geography, History, Linguistics, Philosophy, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, and the Population Institute. To support its students, faculty, staff, and other organizations, it promotes holistic learning by providing spaces to hold academic meetings and events. These spaces are equipped with modern technology and can be configured to meet the specific needs of each group.
